Service

High Scope

High Scope is an approach for early learning which identifies and builds on childrens strengths, interests and abilities. The High Scope approach invites children to be actively invovled in play throughout the session
Fee Structure

Our rates are set in compliance with criteria laid down by the national Children’s Investment Programme (NCIP). We also offer reduced rates to Parents under a range of different schemes, details of which are explained below.
ECCE Scheme

The Growing Tree offers parents of children starting primary school in September 2014 a free childcare place through the Early Childhood Care and Education Scheme. If your child is between the age of 3 years and 2 months and 4 years and seven months on the 1st September 2013 then they are eligible for a place on this scheme.
CCS Programme

Community Childcare Subvention programme is available to parents in receipt of a wide range of social welfare payments (including Family Income Support) and have a medical card. These parents will receive a Band A discount. It is also available to parents are in receipt of a social welfare payment and have no medical card or have just a medical/GP visit card. These parents receive a Band B discount.

CETS Scheme

Childcare Employment and Training Support Programme is available to parents who are participating on specific FAS or VEC courses. There are a limited amount of places on this scheme and if it is determined that a parent is eligible for the CETS programme, childcare costs will be covered for the duration of the parent’s course. If you feel that you are entitled to be on any of at the above schemes, please contact Mary/Roisin. The fee rates from September 2012 to July 2013 are as follows:


  • Band A: €23.65 per week /€4.70 per day
  • Band B: €38.00 per week /€7.60 per day
  • Band C: €55.00 per week / €11.00 per day

Fees are reviewed annually and are set at a rate that ensures continuance of service. Fees are due in full for periods of non-attendance where children are absent due to sickness or away on holidays. N.B – if you are in receipt of subvention and your circumstances change, please make this known to us.
